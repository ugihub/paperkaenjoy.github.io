Shipping Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2025

1. Shipping Information

At Paperka Enjoy, we strive to deliver your premium tobacco products promptly and securely. Please review our shipping policies below to understand our processes and timelines.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

3. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for select locations within a 15-mile radius of our store (orders must be placed before 12 PM)

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary depending on your location and other factors.

4. Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on the weight of your order, shipping method selected, and delivery location:

  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 - $9.99
  • Express Shipping: $12.99 - $19.99
  • Same-Day Delivery: $24.99

Free standard shipping is available for orders over $75.

5. International Shipping

Due to varying regulations regarding tobacco products across different countries, we currently only ship within the United States. We are working to expand our international shipping options in the future.

6. Age Verification

All orders containing tobacco products require age verification upon delivery. The recipient must be at least 21 years of age and present a valid government-issued ID at the time of delivery.

7. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by clicking the tracking link in the email or by logging into your account on our website.
