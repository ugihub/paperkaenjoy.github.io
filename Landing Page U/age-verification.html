Age Verification Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2025

1. Age Requirement

At Paperka Enjoy, we are committed to responsible business practices and strict compliance with all applicable laws regarding the sale of tobacco products. In accordance with federal and state regulations, all visitors to our website and customers of our products must be at least 21 years of age.

2. Website Access

Before accessing our website content, all visitors are required to verify their age through our age verification gateway. This verification process occurs:

  • Upon your first visit to our website
  • When returning to our site after clearing your browser cookies
  • Periodically as required by our compliance procedures

By clicking "I am 21 or older" on our age verification prompt, you confirm that you meet the legal age requirement to view our content and purchase our products.

3. Purchase Verification

In addition to website access verification, we employ a multi-step age verification process for all purchases:

  1. Account Creation: When creating an account, you must provide your date of birth
  2. Order Placement: Our system verifies your age against the information provided
  3. Third-Party Verification: We may use third-party age verification services to validate your age
  4. Delivery Confirmation: Upon delivery, the recipient must present a valid government-issued ID proving they are 21 or older

4. ID Verification

We accept the following forms of identification for age verification purposes:

  • Valid driver's license
  • State-issued identification card
  • Military ID
  • Passport

All identification must be current (not expired) and include a photograph, date of birth, and signature.

5. Failed Verification

If you are unable to verify your age or if our verification process determines you do not meet the age requirement:

  • You will be denied access to our website
  • You will not be permitted to create an account
  • Any attempted purchases will be declined
  • Deliveries will not be completed without proper age verification

6. Purchasing for Minors

It is strictly prohibited to purchase tobacco products from our website with the intent to provide these products to individuals under 21 years of age. Such actions violate federal and state laws and may result in legal consequences.

7. Privacy Considerations

We understand the sensitive nature of personal identification information. All data collected during our age verification process is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and is used solely for the purpose of age verification and compliance with applicable laws.
